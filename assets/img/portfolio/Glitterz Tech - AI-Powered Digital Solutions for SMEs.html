🍽️ In-Restaurant Ordering App – Seamless Dining Experience

👨‍🍳 Client: Taj India - Germany
A fine-dine Indian restaurant in Germany, offering an authentic culinary experience with a modern touch.

🛠 What We Did:

  • ✅ Developed a custom-built in-restaurant ordering app for a smooth dining experience.
  • ✅ Enabled customers to scan a QR code, browse the full menu, place orders, and even request a server.
  • ✅ Designed the app to match the restaurant’s existing branding, ensuring a cohesive aesthetic.
  • ✅ Built a scalable admin portal so the restaurant can easily update menu items, pricing, images, and dietary options—without any coding knowledge.
  • ✅ Implemented a security measure restricting app access to the restaurant’s WiFi, preventing unauthorized external orders.

💡 The Challenge & Our Solution:

TajIndia needed a modern ordering system that improved service efficiency while reducing reliance on waitstaff. The solution had to be intuitive, secure, and fully customizable to their menu offerings.

We built a fully dynamic ordering platform that seamlessly integrates with their workflow, empowering them to manage updates effortlessly while providing customers with an interactive and hassle-free dining experience.

🚀 How We Made the Difference:

Efficiency meets technology—our solution not only optimized customer service but also reduced staffing needs, saving the restaurant valuable time and resources. With a scalable, easy-to-use system, TajIndia now offers a cutting-edge dining experience.
